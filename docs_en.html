Not Chess

🎯 How the Game Works

Win Conditions:

  1. Destroy the opponent's King piece.
  2. Leave the opponent with only their King and Trap (all other pieces destroyed).
  3. The opponents's King is trapped or looses it's ability to move (via Dominant).

Draw:

  1. Only Kings and Traps remain on the board.

⏱ Gameplay

1. Initial Setup:

  • Game board 8×8.
  • Inventory interface: Symbol + piece counter.
  • 24 pieces per player: 21 attacking pieces and 3 special pieces.
  • Players arrange pieces on their 3 rows (Player 1: rows 0–2, Player 2: rows 5–7).
  • Pieces:
    • 👑 1 King
    • 🛡️ 4 Warriors
    • 🧙 4 Wizards
    • 🏹 4 Rangers
    • 🔪 4 Rogues
    • 🙏 4 Monks
    • ⚠️ 1 Mimic
    • 🕳️ 1 Trap
    • 🔁 1 Dominant
  • Buttons:
    • Confirm — verify piece count or end turn.
    • Auto-arrange — randomly arrange pieces. Pressing the button again re-shuffles the pieces.
    • Clean — remove all pieces from the board.
    • Reset — restart the game.
  • Pieces are coloured to match player colour (blue/red).
  • Opponent's pieces are hidden (❓) during setup.

2. Movement:

  • Turn-based gameplay.
  • When selecting a piece the squares for possible turns get highlighted.
  • Pieces can move to one vacant square next to them and to one vacant square diagonally or they can attack the opponent's neighboring pieces.
  • Special pieces can move but cannot attack (except Dominant's effect).
  • Traps are immobile.
  • Players can't attack their own pieces.
  • Opponent's pieces remain hidden (❓) untill attacked.

3. End of the Game:

  • Game ends when any win condition is met.

⚔️ Battle System

  • 🏹 Ranger > 🔪 Rogue, 🙏 Monk.
  • 🔪 Rogue > 🛡️ Warrior, 🧙 Wizard.
  • 🧙 Wizard > 🏹 Ranger, 🛡️ Warrior.
  • 🙏 Monk > 🧙 Wizard, 🔪 Rogue.
  • 🛡️ Warrior > 🏹 Ranger, 🙏 Monk.
  • 👑 King > any opponent's piece if attacks first.
  • 👑 King loses when attaсked.
  • 👑 King steps on a Trap → both pieces disappear → player with remaning King wins.
  • 👑 King attacks opponent's Dominant piece → player loses their ownership over the piece → opponent wins.
  • The same pieces get destroyed when attack each other.

★ Special Pieces

  • ⚠️ Mimic — when a piece attacks Mimic, the attacking piece loses it's ability to move for 2 turns, then Mimic disappeares.
  • 🕳️ Trap — when a piece attacks Trap, both pieces disappear.
  • 🔁 Dominant — when a piece attacks Dominant, the attacking player loses their ability to use the piece. Player with Dominant becomes the new owner of said piece, then Dominant disappears.
